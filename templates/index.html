Elevate Women.
According to UNICEF, 76% of girls in both rural and urban schools are unaware of the proper information on menstruation during adolescence. Elevate Women Period is a trust based in Harare, Zimbabwe focusing on the importance of this education. Elevate Women Period is working with schools around Zimbabwe to provide female hygiene and educational supplies for young women in school. At EWP, we believe the menstrual period is a normal part of life for women. Our hope young girls should never be unable to attend school because of lack of sanitary supplies.
